Japanese Food


A Recipe Is A Story That Ends With A Good Meal

Miso

Miso is a thick, textured paste made by fermenting soybeans with salt and koji (a fermentation culture) as well as rice, barley and other ingredients.

Wasabi

A condiment sometimes referred to as ‘Japanese horseradish’ that is grated from its root into a paste.

Sakura

Extracted from Japan’s signature cherry blossom flower—sakura captures the aromatic and flavour profiles of the flower and leaves.

Tochiotome

Tochigi Prefecture is Japan’s #1 producer of strawberries and produce the country’s best-tasting strawberry—tochiotome!

Yuzu

A lemon-like citrus fruit, yuzu is fantastically fragrant and is prized for its super sour and tart zest and juice.

Matcha

Matcha is a powdered green tea that has a full-bodied leafy flavour ranging from sweet to bitter depending on the quality.

Reshipigyararī レシピギャラリー

Ohagi (Botamochi) おはぎ (ぼたもち)

Made with glutinous rice and red bean paste, They are called Botamochi in spring and Ohagi in autumn.

Chicken Katsu チキンカツ

Serve this classic Japanese food with tonkatsu sauce and a side of fresh salad for the best family meal. It also freezes well and makes for a delicious meal prep menu.

Matcha Tiramisu 抹茶ティラミス

Creamy, rich, and bursting with bold matcha flavors, this irresistible matcha tiramisu consists of matcha soaked ladyfingers layered with a light and airy mascarpone custard filling.
